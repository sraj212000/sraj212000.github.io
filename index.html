PhD PROJECT

Data-Driven Machine Learning Strategies for the Accelerated Discovery of Novel Two-Dimensional Quantum Materials

National Quantum Mission Govt. of India

This project focuses on leveraging high-throughput screening and machine learning interatomic potentials to identify stable 2D materials with exotic quantum properties.
